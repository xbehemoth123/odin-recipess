Lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Cheesse
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Layer the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

Burger

This hamburger patty recipe uses ground beef and an easy bread crumb mixture. Nothing beats a simple hamburger on a warm summer evening! Enjoy on ciabatta, Kaiser, or potato rolls topped with your favorite condiments.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Salt
  • Cheesse
  • Burger bun

Steps

  1. Make the meat and season
  2. Grill the meat
  3. Add cheesse
  4. Grill burger bun
  5. Add sauces to bun
  6. Put the meat in the burger bun
